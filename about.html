About Us

Hyperrealistic Graphite and Charcoal Portraits

Que's Art Studio: Founded in 2024.

The home of hyperrealistic charcoal and graphite portraits - Qhama Hlana

  • Founder

    Qhama Hlana - "I am the founder of the business and I don't have a team but I work alone, with the art handling material and everything including delivery.

  • Establishment

    Our business was founded last year in October, after I gave my friend a portrait for her birthday as a gift. From then more people saw hher portrait and asked for their own so I decided to start a business and sell my art

  • The Ordering Process

    Each portrait has it's on price depending on the size and the amount of people you want me to fit into the portrait. After discussing that we ask a 50% deposit

  • The Art Process

    After we get the deposit. You just simple send me your pictures and after a maximum of 2-3 weeks. We deliver the portrait at your doorstep


For the best results, portraits take a minimum of a week to 2 weeks. It takes this long because as artist, we can't rush the process. Patients is what produces the hyper realisim.

Business Information:

We produce the best art portraits we can to keep our customers satisfied and fufilled. Portraits all come framed and wrapped so you can gift it to your friends, or have it framed in your home.
